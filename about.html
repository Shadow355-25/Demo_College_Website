Our History

Mahatma Ghandhi Memorial College was founded in 1949 and is one of the largest and most respected colleges in Karnataka.Named after the Father of the Nation, the college carries forward a legacy of truth,service, and knowledge.

In accordance with the karnataka Government order,MGM College has been bifurcated into Mahatma Gandhi Memorial College and Mahatma Gandhi Memorial Pre-University College, both operating on the same campus.

Vision & Mission

Our Motto: "Sathwath Sanjayathe Jnanam" — From virtue springs knowledge (Bhagavad Geetha).

Vision: To be a centre of excellence that nurtures intellectual growth, ethical values, and social responsibility in every student.

Mission:

🔹 Provide quality education with modern infrastructure.

🔹 Foster research, innovation, and critical thinking.

🔹 Build character through discipline and cultural values.

🔹 Empower students to contribute to society and the nation.

Principal's Message

Management
