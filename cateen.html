Annapoorna Canteen



  1. Canteen was inaugurated by Dr. M I Savadati (Vice-chancellor, Mangalore university) on 23rd March, 1990.
  2. Another area where the college has come to the help of students is in providing healthy mid-day meals for the day scholars in the canteen.
  3. The new building Annapoorna presents today a delightful scene at noon when more than 400 students and teachers take advantage of the subsidized food.
  4. This facility available in the campus makes the task of organizing whole day functions like seminars and workshops less cumbersome.
